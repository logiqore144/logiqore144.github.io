Privacy Policy

Simple, honest data protection

Back to Calculator

The Bottom Line: LogiQore is a UK company that helps optimize Microsoft Sentinel costs. We only collect data you give us through our calculator and contact forms. We don't sell your data, send spam, or share it with anyone without permission. Your data stays in the UK/EU and you can ask us to delete it anytime.

What We Collect

When you use our cost calculator or contact us, we collect:

  • Your name, email, and company details
  • Technical information about your Microsoft Sentinel setup
  • Website usage data (which pages you visit, how long you spend)

How We Use It

  • Provide you with cost savings calculations
  • Schedule and conduct technical consultation calls
  • Improve our calculator and website
  • Respond to your questions

We don't: Send marketing emails unless you ask, sell your data to anyone, or use it for anything other than helping with your Microsoft Sentinel costs.

Where Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored in the UK using Microsoft Azure (UK South region). Contact forms are processed by Formspree (EU-based). Your data never leaves the UK/EU.

Your Rights

You can ask us to:

  • Send you a copy of your data
  • Correct any mistakes
  • Delete your data completely
  • Stop processing your data

Contact Us

For any privacy questions or to exercise your rights:
Email: info@logiqore.com
Response time: Within 30 days

If you're not happy with how we handle your data, you can complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk
